Thanksgiving in the USA

It originated as a harvest festival and has been celebrated nationally on and off since 1789.

The First Thanksgiving

This feast at Plymouth lasted three days, and was attended by 90 Native Americans and 53 Pilgrims.

Thanksgiving Day Service

This service held for US Army Air Corps in a church in Cransley, Northamptonshire, England, November 23, 1944.

Pardoning Turkeys Tradition

The practice of pardoning turkeys by President John F. Kennedy in this manner became a permanent tradition in 1989.

Vacation and Dinner

On Thanksgiving Day, families and friends usually gather for a large meal or dinner.
